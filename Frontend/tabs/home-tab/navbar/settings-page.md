Now, lets break down the settings page. In the top left corner, like most popup windows, there is an "x".

Then centered, there is a menu. In this menu there is 1 toggle for a white or dark mode. And 2 submenus "API Keys" & "Account Info". On the "API key submenu" there is a list with the name of the tool, and the api key, and on the account info menu, at the top... there is a button to link the instagram account, highlighted from everything, and then, as a list, there is the phone, email and name (first and last seperately).
