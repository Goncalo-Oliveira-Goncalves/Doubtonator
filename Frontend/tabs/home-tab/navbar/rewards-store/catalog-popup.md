# Catalog Popup Window

> *Copied from: Frontend.md — "Catalog Popup Window"*

> Demo: [catalog-popup.html](catalog-popup.html) ← empty stub

This popup shall be very simple to create, since it reutilizes elements from other windows. There are 2 sections; Display & Catalog, the display section is an exact structural copy of the one on the [item-popup.md](item-popup.md), instead of a product being shown however, it's an image of choice & the png of a shadow.

This image of choice will be set on the backend. Manually, by me.

The catalog section is literally a list version of that product card presented on the rewards store, the one on section 2, it's that card repeated, and with different colors, values, and images, this is because they are from the same database. And the interactivity is the same; Upon clicking, takes you to the item popout window.
