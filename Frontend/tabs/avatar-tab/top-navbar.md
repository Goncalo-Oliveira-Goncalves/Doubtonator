# Top Navbar

> *Copied from: Frontend.md — "Top Navbar" (avatar tab)*

> Demo: [profile-card.html](profile-card.html) ← empty stub (card subtab)

The first icon on the leftmost of the container, a container containing the 3, a container with about 75% of the width of the screen and centered, will be a node tree, the second icon, at the center of the container, will be a sort of card, kinds of like an ID, the third icon on the rightmost of the container will be a book, like a journal.

Now let's go over the subtabs they represent. (yes, they are subtabs, not same functionality as the home page, but more like the clan.)

The node tree represents your progress in real life, increase in views per week, followers per day, etc... It is basically an evolution analysis dashboard, which we'll go over in a bit.
The card is basically sort of like a passport, it will have their name, their level, their progress towards the next level (an in a progress bar with XP), recordings done, their streak, their sparks, their rank, and their achievements.
The book is where the journaling feature comes in.

The default subtab's the card. By the way, the top nav has a smooth transition to the content, so it's not visible, there's no hard dividing line here.

And by the way, the icon is literal node tree, and is the git branches, that is what it looks like, while a canvas is a map. Totally different.
